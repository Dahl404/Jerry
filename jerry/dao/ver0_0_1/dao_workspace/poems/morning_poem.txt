The sun crests over hills of gold,
A gentle warmth to softly unfold.
The birds begin their morning song,
A melody that I can long.

The dewdrops dance upon the grass,
A silver morning's precious pass.
The world awakens, breathes anew,
With hope and beauty all around.

The coffee cup, the quiet page,
The quiet hours that we wage.
The gentle breeze, the soft refrain,
Of life's first promise, first refrain.

So let the morning bring its grace,
With every breath, a fresh embrace.
The world is new, the world is bright,
A canvas painted in the light.
