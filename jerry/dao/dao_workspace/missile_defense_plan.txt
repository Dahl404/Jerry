MISSION: PLAY MISSILE DEFENSE GAME AND GET 100 POINTS
=====================================================

STRATEGY:
- Launch missiles continuously (they respawn)
- Use interceptors to destroy them before they hit
- Interceptors cost points, missiles destroy points
- Goal: 100 points total

PLAYSTYLE:
1. Start the game
2. Watch missiles fall from sky
3. Click/tap to launch interceptors
4. Destroy missiles before they hit the ground
5. Accumulate points
6. Repeat until 100 points

NOTE: This is a rapid-fire game requiring quick reflexes!
