
When day retreats and twilight comes,
A golden light in silence sums.
The stars begin to whisper low,
Above the earth where shadows grow.

The moon ascends, a silver glow,
To guide the night and watch it flow.
The world is still, a peaceful scene,
Where dreams begin and secrets mean.

So let the evening bring its grace,
With quiet beauty, soft embrace.
The day is done, the night is new,
A canvas painted in the blue.
